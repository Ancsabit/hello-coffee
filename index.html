Types of Instant Coffee

Spray-Dried: Uses hot air to evaporate water, resulting in fine, powdery granules. Cheaper but may have a less rich flavor.
Freeze-Dried: Freezes coffee extract and removes ice through sublimation, preserving flavor and aroma. Generally higher quality.
